Meet Your Agent

Sam Carchidi brings real-world experience and local insight to every real estate deal. With a background in mortgage processing, he understands both the financial and emotional sides of buying or selling a home—helping clients move forward with clarity and confidence. Having lived in Philadelphia for over a decade and previously in Wenonah and Ocean City, New Jersey, Sam offers a well-rounded perspective on city living, the suburbs, and the shore. Now licensed in both PA and NJ, he works with first-time buyers, seasoned investors, and everyone in between. His philosophy is simple: Providing my clients the same care and compassion that I put into my everyday relationships—because buying or selling a home isn’t just a transaction, it’s a major life moment. When he’s not working with clients, you’ll likely find Sam catching a show in the local music scene or rooting for the Eagles and Phillies—always staying connected to the communities he serves.
